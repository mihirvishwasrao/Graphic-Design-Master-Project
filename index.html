In this graphic design master project, the chosen focus was The Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Agra. The objectives were: firstly, to design a diverse array of elements—New Yorker ads, a letterhead, an information page, an envelope, a business card, a website landing page, a mobile application logo, delivery van design, and landscape site signs—each showcasing the majestic essence of The Taj Mahal. Moreover, the project aimed to intricately unify these distinct pieces, achieving a seamless synchronization. Ultimately, the design was presented through a comprehensive summary sheet and a booklet.
